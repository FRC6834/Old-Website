Our History


FIRST Team #6834 was founded in 2017 by Rob Adams, Emory George, Dale Fife, and Melissa Ladowitz. Our mission is to deliver an enriching learning experience for every member of the team. Our students are a part of a community dedicated to helping them succeed at building different forms of technology. The experience will strengthen the students' teamwork, business, building, and programming skills.
